Common Enquiries

What is the registration fee for ESAT? expand_more

The ESAT is currently free of charge for students registering early to encourage scientific aptitude across the region.

Is ESAT based on the school syllabus? expand_more

Yes, the syllabus is aligned with the CBSE/RBSE curriculum for the respective class level, with a slight emphasis on analytical thinking and basic concepts.

How will I receive my scholarship? expand_more

Scholarships are awarded as tuition fee waivers upon successful admission to the Elementary Science Academy for the upcoming academic session.
